The California Social Work Education Center (CalSWEC), the nation’s largest state coalition of social work educators and practitioners, is based at the School of Social Welfare at the University of California Berkeley. Its programmatic areas are child welfare, mental health, and aging. The main phone number is 510-642-9272.

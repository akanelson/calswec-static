2014 Aging Initiative Summit

On the Road: Preparing Social Workers for an Interprofessional Environment was the theme of the 2014 Aging Initiative Summit held on February 5 in Sacramento. (Read the preliminary story about the summit, "2014 Aging Summit: Preparing Social Workers for an Interprofessional Environment.")

Goals

The goals of the 2014 summit were: 

  • To build on the work of the 2012 Aging Initiative Summit that introduced the changing landscape of health care delivery for older adults.
     
  • To bring stakeholders and partners together for a working session to review existing curricula and field work; identify gaps; and make recommendations that will be consolidated into a working action plan for enhanced curricula and field work that prepare social workers serving an aging population in an interprofessional environment.

2012 Aging Initiative Summit

On September 19, 2012, CalSWEC hosted a summit themed "The Road Ahead: Advocating for Social Work Service Delivery in Aging," which brought together a diverse group of leaders in gerontological social work practice and education. (Read an article about the summit, "Inaugural Agining Initiative Summit Gets Down to Business.")

Goals
The goals of the summit were:

  • To articulate a vision of the Workforce of the Future that is needed to respond to the changing landscape of service delivery for older adults in a health care- driven system
  • To highlight collaborative environments where social workers are effective members of cross-disciplinary teams serving older adults

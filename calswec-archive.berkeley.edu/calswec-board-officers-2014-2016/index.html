CalSWEC Board Officers for 2014–2016

Warning message

You are currently visiting the archived CalSWEC website. To return to the new CalSWEC website at any time, go to https://calswec.berkeley.edu.

CalSWEC’s newly elected Board of Directors officers for 2014–2016 are:

President Scott Gruendl, director, Glenn County Health and Human Services Agency; Vice President Amy Levin, associate professor and chair, Department of Social Work at California State University, Northridge (both pictured at  right); Secretary Elliott Robinson, director, Monterey County Department of Social Services, also chair of the board’s Workforce Development and Funding Committee; and Treasurer Ronnie Swartz, chair, Humboldt State University Department of Social Work.

Other members of the Board of Directors Executive Committee are:

  • Immediate Past Board President Celeste Jones, director, CSU, Chico School of Social Work;
  • Curriculum and In-Service Training Committee Chair Nancy Meyer-Adams, associate professor and director, CSU, Long Beach School of Social Work;
  • Research and Development Committee Chair Virginia Rondero Hernandez, professor and director, CSU, Fresno Department of Social Work Education;
  • Aging Committee Chair Laurie Smith, director, CSU, San Bernardino School of Social Work;
  • Mental Health Committee Chair Jo Ann Johnson, program manager, Sacramento County Division of Behavioral Health Services;
  • Child Welfare Committee Chair Lori Cox, director, Alameda County Social Services; and
  • Principal Investigator Jeffrey Edleson, dean, UC Berkeley School of Social Welfare.

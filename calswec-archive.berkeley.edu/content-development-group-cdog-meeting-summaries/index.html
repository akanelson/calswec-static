Content Development Group (CDOG) Meeting Summaries

Warning message

You are currently visiting the archived CalSWEC website. To return to the new CalSWEC website at any time, go to https://calswec.berkeley.edu.

Members of the Content Development Oversight Group (CDOG), a subcommittee of the Statewide Training and Education Committee (STEC), participate in monthly conference calls and semi-annual in-person meetings.

For more information:

  • Contact: Jennifer Cannell, Training and Curriclum Specialist

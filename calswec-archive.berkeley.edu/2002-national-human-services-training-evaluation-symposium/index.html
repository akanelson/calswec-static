2002 National Human Services Training Evaluation Symposium

Warning message

You are currently visiting the archived CalSWEC website. To return to the new CalSWEC website at any time, go to https://calswec.berkeley.edu.

May 22-24, 2002 | University of California, Berkeley

The Fifth Annual National Human Services Training Evaluation Symposium addressed the spectrum of issues directly tied to training evaluation, including:

  • Evaluation design issues,
  • Selection and development of instrumentation,
  • Data collection,
  • Analysis, report writing,
  • Dissemination,
  • Use of evaluation findings.
  • The purposes and uses of training evaluation in relation to desired agency outcomes, and
  • Working with policy and program staff to plan useful evaluation.

"Advancing Implementation of Pathways to Well-Being: Integration and Sharing to Improve Outcomes"

August 21, 2014 | 10:00 am–4:00 pm, Doubletree Hotel, Sacramento

  • Day-at-a-Glance
  • Detailed Agenda (Download or print PDF version)
  • Keynote Speaker Dr. Joan Pennell

DETAILED AGENDA

10:00–10:15 am

Welcome

  • Barrett Johnson, Director,  In-Service Training, California Social Work Education Center

5 LeadershipTracks: Interactive Breakout Sessions

Track 1—Policy: Implementation of Intensive Care Coordination (ICC) and Intensive Home Based Services (IHBS) Challenges to Implementation and Sample Solutions

  • Rich Weisgal, M.A., LMFT, Consultant, Shared Vision Consultants 
    Handouts: Implementation of Intensive Care Coordination (ICC) and Intensive Home Based Services (IHBS)    ; Sample County Tools for Katie A. Implementation; Katie A. Mental Health Services Report

    Handout: Integrated Care in Placer County

Track 3—Teaming: Promoting and Implementing Successful Family Teams: Peer Learning

  • David Foster, LCSW, Director, Central California and Bay Area Training Academies, Facilitator
  • Patricia Poulsen, M.S.W., Central California Training Academy, Facilitator
  • Maria Salinas, Countywide Parent Advocate; Child Welfare Division, Department of Mental Health Wraparound County Administration, Los Angeles County
  • Hector Cerda, M.S.W., Central Valley Regional Coordinator, California Youth Connection (CYC)
  • Jason Lowe, Chairperon of the California Youth Connection, Calaveras County Chapter
  • Don Pickens, Program Director & Leadership Coordinator, Parents Anonymous© Inc.
  • Michelle Allen, Glenn County Parents Anonymous Parent Leader
    Handouts: Pathways to Mental Health Services Core Practice Model Guide—Teaming; MHSD Information Notice No. 13-13; CCTA LCAP Recommendations; CCTA LCFF Recommendations 4-30-14 Updated; Letter to FUSD Board President Valerie Davis

Track 4—Screening and Assessment: Implementing Screening and Assessment Practices in Your County

  • John Landsverk, Ph.D., Research Scientist, Oregon Social Learning Center, Facilitator
  • Natalie Bolin, LCSW, Manager, Tulare County Child Welfare Services,
  • Brent Crandal, Ph.D., Grant Psychologist, California Screening, Assessment, and Treatment (CSAT) Initiative
  • Nathaniel Israel, Ph.D., Policy Fellow, Chapin Hall at the University of Chicago
  • Jesse Russell, Ph.D., Director of Research, National Center on Crime and Delinquency and the Children's Research Center
  • Dorian Traube, Ph.D., Associate Professor, University of Southern California School of Social Work

Track 5—Strategic Planning: Strategic Planning and the Importance of Collaboration in the Implementation of Pathways to Well-Being (Katie A.)

  • Barrett Johnson, LCSW, Director, In-Service Training, California Social Work Education Center (CalSWEC)
  • Lisa Molinar, M.A., President, Shared Vision Consultants
